7 PM · Metro · Goodbye
End of Day · Shibuya Station

She broke down
on the metro.

You were laidback about it all day. Both of you. You accepted the return. You were fine at the crepe shop, fine at the sky, fine at the cherry blossoms.

Then 7 PM came, and she was dropping you off, and the metro doors were closing and something shifted. She cried. You got sad too.

You promised to meet again later in life. That's not nothing. That's everything.
