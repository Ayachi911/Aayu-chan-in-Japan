★ FEATURED ★

Japan Exchange Year (Age 16) 🌸

Asia Kakehashi Scholar 2021 • Full Year

i was ONE OF 21 students from india (250 from all of asia!!!) selected by the japanese government for a year-long cultural exchange. lived with a host family in yokohama, went to yokohama hiranuma high school, learned kyuudo (japanese archery!), tea ceremony, calligraphy, and competed in sports. scored 2nd in the ENTIRE SCHOOL on the english exam as a foreign student!! this changed my whole life tbh

#cultural-ambassador #japan #scholarship #life-changing
