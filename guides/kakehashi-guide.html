What is Asia Kakehashi Project?

It's a fully-funded exchange program run by the Japanese government where you get to visit Japan for a full year, attend a Japanese high school, and live with a host family.

The best part? It's completely free â€” they cover your flights, accommodation, meals, and even give you a monthly stipend.

Who Can Apply?

Here's what you need:

  • Age between 15-18 years old
  • Currently enrolled as a student in India
  • Able to communicate in English (you don't need to be fluent)
  • Genuinely interested in Japanese culture and international exchange
